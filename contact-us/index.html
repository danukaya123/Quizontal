Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get a response?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours. For urgent matters marked as "High" priority, we aim to respond within 1-2 hours during business hours (Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 6 PM GMT+5:30).

What information should I include in my message?

Please include:

  • Your name and contact information
  • Detailed description of your inquiry or issue
  • URL of the page you're having issues with (if applicable)
  • Screenshots or error messages (for technical issues)
  • Your browser and device information (for technical support)

Do you offer partnership opportunities?

Yes! We're always open to partnerships with content creators, developers, and businesses. Please select "Partnership Opportunity" as your subject in the contact form and provide details about your proposal. We review all partnership inquiries and respond to promising opportunities within 3-5 business days.

How can I report a bug or technical issue?

Please select "Bug Report" as your subject and include:

  • Steps to reproduce the issue
  • Expected vs actual behavior
  • Screenshots or screen recordings
  • Your browser, device, and OS information
  • Any error messages you received
This helps us quickly identify and fix the issue.

Can I request a new feature for Quizontal?

Absolutely! We love hearing feature requests from our users. Please select "Feature Request" as your subject and provide as much detail as possible about:

  • The problem you're trying to solve
  • How you envision the feature working
  • Why this would be valuable for you and other users
  • Any examples from other platforms (if applicable)
We review all feature requests and add popular ones to our development roadmap.
