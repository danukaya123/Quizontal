Introduction

Welcome to Quizontal ("we," "our," or "us"). We operate the website quizontal.com (the "Service") which provides:

  • High-quality stock photos, images, and videos
  • AI-powered creative tools (Image Generator, Background Remover, Text Paraphraser, Text Humanizer)

  • Utility tools (Calculators, SEO tools, JS Obfuscator, Text tools)
  • Wallpapers and creative resources

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services. Please read it carefully.

Information We Collect

Note: We are committed to minimal data collection. Most of our services can be used without providing personal information.

Personal Information

Automatically Collected Information

When you access our Service, we automatically collect certain information:

Data Type Purpose Examples
Usage Data Improve service quality Pages visited, time spent, features used
Device Information Optimize user experience Browser type, device type, operating system

Cookies Data Enhance functionality Session management, preferences

AI Tools Processing

When using our AI tools (Image Generator, Text Paraphraser, etc.):

  • Text prompts and inputs are processed temporarily for generating results
  • Uploaded images for background removal are processed in real-time
  • We do not store or retain your AI-generated content permanently
  • All processing happens in memory and is discarded after completion

We use the collected information for the following purposes:
